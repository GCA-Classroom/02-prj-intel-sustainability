Intel's Sustainability Goals

Building a greener future through innovation and responsibility.

1968–1999
1968: Intel is founded by Robert Noyce and Gordon Moore in Mountain View, California.
2000–2010
2003: Board-level oversight for corporate responsibility.
2020–2025
2020: Launched the RISE 2030 strategy: Responsible, Inclusive, Sustainable, Enabling.
2030
2030: 100% renewable electricity, 60B gallons water conserved, zero waste to landfill, 10x energy efficiency.
2040
2040: Net-zero emissions globally.
2050
2050: Supplier-level net-zero emissions and 100% renewable energy adoption.
Intel is aiming not just for compliance, but for leadership in sustainable innovation across the entire tech ecosystem.
